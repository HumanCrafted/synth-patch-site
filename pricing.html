Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I hit 20 patches on the Free plan?

You'll need to delete some patches to create new ones, or upgrade to Pro for unlimited patches. Your existing patches are never deleted automatically.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade to Pro at any time. If you downgrade from Pro to Free, you'll keep all your patches but won't be able to create new ones if you have more than 20 until you delete some.

How does annual billing work?

With annual billing, you pay only $60/year instead of $8/month ($96/year), saving you $36. You'll be billed once per year and can cancel anytime, with access until the end of your billing period.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll continue to have Pro access until the end of your current billing period.
