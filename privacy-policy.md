# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** October 13, 2025

**Last Updated:** October 13, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Synth Patch, a service operated by Human Crafted LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our web application for creating, managing, and sharing synthesizer patches.

---

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Display name
- Authentication credentials (managed securely by Clerk)
- Account creation date and last login time

### User-Generated Content

When you use Synth Patch, we store:
- Synthesizer patch configurations (knob positions, cable connections, component states)
- Patch names, descriptions, and notes
- Audio files you upload to demonstrate patches
- Remix relationships (when you create variations of existing patches)
- Star/favorite preferences

### Usage Information

We automatically collect through Vercel Analytics:
- Pages visited and features used
- Browser type and device information
- General location (country/region only, no precise location)
- Session duration and interaction patterns
- Referral sources

Note: We do not directly collect or store IP addresses. Vercel may process IP addresses temporarily for analytics aggregation but does not provide them to us.

### Marketing Information

If you opt-in to communications:
- Email address
- Account status and activity
- Email engagement metrics (managed by Loops)

Note: During our beta period, we use a waitlist system. Once we open to the public, email communications will be opt-in only.

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Data
- **Application Hosting:** Vercel (United States)
- **Database:** Neon PostgreSQL (United States)
- **File Storage:** Vercel Blob Storage (United States)
- **Email Platform:** Loops (United States)

### How We Protect Data
- All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Authentication tokens are securely managed by Clerk
- Database access is restricted and monitored
- Regular security updates and dependency patches
- Access controls limit data exposure to authorized systems only

### Data Retention
- Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely while account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days of account deletion
- Backups: Managed by our hosting providers (Vercel and Neon) according to their retention policies

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access Your Data
You can view and download your patch data at any time through the Patch Data panel in the patch editor.

### Delete Your Account
You can delete your account through Clerk's account settings. Upon deletion:
- Your Clerk account is immediately removed
- You are automatically removed from email marketing lists
- Your patches and content remain on the platform for attribution purposes (original creator credit on remixes)

To request complete data deletion including all patches and uploaded files, contact us at hello@synthpatch.io. We will manually remove your content within 30 days.

### Opt-Out of Marketing
- Update your email preferences in account settings
- Click "unsubscribe" in any marketing email

### Export Your Data
Use the "Copy JSON" feature in the Patch Data panel to export your patch configurations.

## Public Content

### Shared Patches
When you mark a patch as "Public":
- It appears on the Explore page for all users
- The share URL is accessible without authentication
- Your creator name is visible on the patch
- Anyone can view (but not edit) the patch configuration

### Remix Attribution
When you remix another user's patch:
- Original creator attribution is preserved
- Remix relationships are publicly visible
- You cannot claim original authorship
